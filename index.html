Musical Statement (Demonstration)

Idea behind the Node Sequencer
- Create nodes and playheads through the trackpad.
- In node mode, tapping the touchpad creates nodes.
- In playhead mode, swiping the touchpad creates playhead with the direction and velocity of the swipe.
- Sends OSC to Max for Live to render the sound.
